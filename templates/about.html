About Me

Hi! I'm Kevin. I build the foundational systems that turn raw data into clear insights and intelligent applications.

To put my skills into practice, I recently engineered a complete data platform from scratch, analyzing the impact of weather on NYC taxi trips. This project involved the full data lifecycle: I used Terraform to automate cloud infrastructure on AWS, built a robust ELT pipeline with Airflow and dbt to process and test the data, and developed an interactive dashboard for the final analysis.

My Master's in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology, with a focus on Machine Learning, gave me a deep appreciation for the end goal: using clean, reliable data to build powerful analytics and predictive models. I'm passionate about the entire journey, from raw data ingestion to the final, actionable insight.

I'm currently seeking an entry-level role in Data Engineering, Data Analytics, or Machine Learning where I can contribute to building impactful, data-driven solutions. Let's connect and build something great together!
